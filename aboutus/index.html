About Us

Founded in 2007 in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, Shree Sainath Packaging is a trusted manufacturer of high-quality packaging solutions. With over a decade of experience, we specialize in BOPP bags, woven sack bags, and non-woven bags, catering to a wide range of industrial and commercial packaging needs.

Our focus on quality, durability, and client satisfaction has made us a reliable partner for businesses across sectors. Despite being a small team, we deliver big results through commitment, craftsmanship, and attention to detail.

What We Strive For

At Shree Sainath Packaging, we are driven by a deep commitment to helping businesses pack their products with confidence. Every bag we manufacture is a result of careful material selection, precision manufacturing, and a dedication to consistency.

We believe packaging is more than just wrapping — it's about protecting value, representing a brand, and making a lasting impression. Our goal is to deliver solutions that not only meet practical needs but also enhance the identity and trust our clients build with their customers.

Our Vision

To become a leading name in the Indian packaging industry by continuously innovating, expanding our product range, and building long-term relationships with our customers through trust and excellence.

To be recognized for our contribution to responsible manufacturing and our commitment to creating packaging solutions that are both efficient and environmentally conscious.

  • Customer Commitment: We go the extra mile to exceed customer expectations.
